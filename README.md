# sauce-lab
An app that organizes, stores, and helps you perfect your homemade hot sauce recipes.

SauceLab organizes, stores, and helps you perfect your homemade hot sauce recipes. Track ingredients, monitor heat levels, and leave notes all in one place.
            
## How to use
Fill in the recipe details below. Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*). You can save your recipe as "Work in Progress" and come back to perfect it later.
