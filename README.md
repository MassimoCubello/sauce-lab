# sauce-lab
SauceLab organizes, stores, and helps you perfect your homemade hot sauce recipes. Track ingredients, monitor heat levels, and leave notes all in one place.
            
## How to use
Fill in the recipe details. Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*). You can save your recipe as "Work in Progress" and come back to perfect it later.

## Features
- Add and edit recipes with ease.
- Track ingredients and their quantities.
- Monitor heat levels and spice ratings.
